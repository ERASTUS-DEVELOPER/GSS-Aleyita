Welcome to our school
Government Secondary School Aleyita
Government Secondary School, Aleyita is a mixed secondary school that was established in the year 2025. The school was founded to provide quality education to both boys and girls, helping
them develop academically and socially in a supportive learning environment.

The school is located in Aleyita along Airport Road, Abuja. This location makes the school
easily accessible and places it in a peaceful area that is suitable for teaching and learning activities.

Government Secondary School, Aleyita places great importance on academic excellence.
Teachers are dedicated to delivering quality instruction that helps students gain knowledge, skills, and confidence needed for further education and future careers.

In addition to academics, the school emphasizes good morals and the fear of God. Students
are taught values such as honesty, discipline, respect, and responsibility, which help shape their character and behavior both in and outside the school.

The school is driven by strong values that guide all its actions and decisions. These values
support the school’s vision of raising well-rounded students who are academically sound, morally upright, and prepared to contribute positively to society.
